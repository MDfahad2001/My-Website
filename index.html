Who I Am

I'm an electronics technician and embedded systems developer currently based in London, Ontario. I completed my diploma in Electronics and Embedded Systems Development at Fanshawe College, building on a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from India.

I specialize in microcontroller firmware (C/C++), hardware debugging, and serial protocol implementation across UART, SPI, I2C, and CAN. I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software — from schematic reading and PCB layout to firmware bring-up and IoT integration.

When I'm not building things, I'm learning new tools. Currently exploring Qt, MATLAB, and CODESys for industrial automation environments.
